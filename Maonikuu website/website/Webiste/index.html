Maonikuu Creative Studios

Maonikuu creatives is a full service production, events, brand communications and social media strategist firm based in Kenya. We bring a fresh, unique approach to the industry by applying strong management, analytical and problem-solving skills.

Laser sharp focus, unparalleled professionalism and exceptional service means clients can trust that their project is in capable hands.
